MIT Learn Course Spot Check Tool

Automated course quality checks for MITx Online

What This Tool Does

This tool checks your course for common mistakes in settings and content. However, it cannot replace beta testing for a thorough test of your course material, or a pair of human eyes to make sure that everything is set as you need it to be.

How to Use

  1. Navigate to your course in Studio
  2. Go to "Tools" and click "Export"
  3. Download the tarball file to your computer
  4. Upload the file using the form below

⏱️ Processing Time: Processing files may take at least 5 minutes, longer for larger courses. Please be patient while your course is being analyzed.
